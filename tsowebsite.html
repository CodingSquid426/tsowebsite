About Us

The Turl Street Orchestra (TSO) is a vibrant, non-auditioning student orchestra that rehearses weekly at Exeter College Chapel in Oxford. We welcome musicians of all abilities to join us every Friday evening during term time. Our mission is to bring together students from diverse musical backgrounds to enjoy playing classical music in a friendly, relaxed setting.

Rehearsals

Rehearsals take place at Exeter College Chapel on Fridays from 7:30 PM to 10:00 PM during term time (Weeks 1 to 7). We focus on playing beautiful classical pieces, such as Gluck's Iphigenia in Aulis or Mozart's Symphony No. 38, while ensuring a fun and collaborative environment. Snacks, including biscuits, are always available during breaks!

Concerts

TSO performs three concerts per year, one at the end of each academic term. The concerts are held at Exeter College Chapel and are completely free to attend. Our concerts are approximately one hour in length and provide an opportunity for the entire community to come and enjoy our music. Keep an eye on our website and social media for concert dates!
